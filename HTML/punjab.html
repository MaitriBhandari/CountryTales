Himachal Pradesh and Punjab (6 days and 5 nights)


Himachal Pradesh and Punjab are one of the most popular states of India. The fertile land of farms and forts, Punjab has been aptly nicknamed the 'Smiling Soul of India' whereas Himachal is known for its adventure index.html activities like ice skating in Shimla, paragliding in Bir Billing and Solang valley, rafting in Kullu, skiing in Manali, horse riding and fishing in different parts in the state.

Major Attractions:

Jallianwala Bagh, Wagah Border, Shimla, Kasauli, Dharamshala, Manali, Rock Garden

Famous Wildlife Sanctuaries:

Bandli Wildlife Sanctuary, Bir Moti Bagh Wildlife Sanctuary, Abohar Wildlife Sanctuary, Chail Wildlife Sanctuary
