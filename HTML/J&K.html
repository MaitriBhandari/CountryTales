Jammu and Kashmir (6 days and 7 nights)


Known as Heaven on the Earth, Jammu & Kashmir is where Mother Nature has bestowed all her love. Whatever your reason for visiting J&K, whether it is to enjoy the beauty, or test your limits while trekking the mountains, or simply to discover serenity and peace, J&K will offer you several reasons to keep coming back for more.

Major Attractions:

Yusmarg, Gulmarg, Verinag, Mughal Gardens, Doda, Ladakh, Indira Gandhi Memorial Tulip Garden, Dachigam National Park

Famous Lakes:

Dal Lake, Pangong Tso, Vishansar Lake, Gadsar Lake, Sheshnag, Wular Lake, Tulian Lake
